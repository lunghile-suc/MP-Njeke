Summary of Book

Precisely the book seeks to advance the understanding and often misconstrued concept of Christianity, traversing two critical gospel mindsets of inclusivism versus exclusivism. The book is delving into intricacies and paradoxes of the Christian gospel trying to find equilibrium. Depending on who controls and directs your mind – could thus determine your Christian fate.

This book explains, who we let control our minds- using his past experiences – obviously, he can destroy or redeem your life – or saves us from apocalyptic quagmire or can contributes to our calamity.

Thus, this book will help you to:

- Nevigate your life according to God’s wisdom, and will rekindle God’s love in you through its different comprehensive teachings.

- Re-discover the power of love and the fruits of dedication.

The book is designed to push your limits of Christian understanding -guaranteeing that the time you finish reading – you should distinguish inter-alia - who the atheist is, Christian and a Born-Again Christian.
